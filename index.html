Welcome to the Kingdom Desk Reference App

This KDR Gold Edition app is your daily Kingdom lifestyle companion. Use the A-Series to align your heart and identity, the S-Series to anchor yourself in Scripture, and the P-Series to practice Kingdom living through daily actions. You can search for words like “worship,” “trust,” “peace,” or “forgiveness” to quickly find the card you need in the moment.
